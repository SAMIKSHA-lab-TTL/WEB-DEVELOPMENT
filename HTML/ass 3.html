My Healthy Morning Routine

Starting the day right sets the tone for everything ahead. Here's what I do every morning to feel energized and productive.

Step-by-Step Routine

  1. Wake up at 6:00 AM
  2. Drink a glass of water
  3. Do 15 minutes of stretching
  4. Go for a 30-minute walk
  5. Take a shower and get dressed
  6. Eat a healthy breakfast

Healthy Breakfast Options

  • Oatmeal with fruits
  • Scrambled eggs with veggies
  • Smoothie with protein and greens
  • Whole grain toast with peanut butter

Key Terms

Hydration
The process of absorbing water, essential after a night's sleep.
Stretching
Light exercises to loosen up the body and improve flexibility.
Mindfulness
A mental state achieved by focusing on the present moment.
